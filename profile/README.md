## Global SaaS Engineering Partners
<img src="https://github.com/SleekDigital-WorkShop/.github/blob/main/profile/SleekDigital.png" align="right" alt="SleekDigital logo" width="150" height="150">

*SleekDigital* is a Singapore‑headquartered software engineering firm specializing exclusively in the design, development, and scaling of SaaS platforms for clients across North America, Europe, Asia‑Pacific, and the Middle East.

We are technology‑agnostic by principle. No prescribed frameworks. No vendor lock‑in. Our engineering teams select the optimal stack for each product—whether TypeScript, Python, Go, or Rust; monolith or microservices; serverless or dedicated infrastructure; cloud‑native or hybrid.

Our capabilities span the full SaaS lifecycle: architecture design, full‑stack engineering, multi‑region scaling, enterprise security (GDPR, PDPA, CCPA readiness), AI integration (LLM pipelines, RAG systems, agentic workflows), and ongoing product evolution.

We have delivered over 100 SaaS platforms across FinTech, HealthTech, Logistics, PropTech, Retail, EdTech, HRTech, Energy, and Industrial IoT—serving regulated enterprises, funded startups, and growth‑stage scale‑ups alike.

Clients retain full IP ownership. No licensing fees. No ongoing royalties. We build what you own.
